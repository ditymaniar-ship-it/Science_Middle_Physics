Force & Motion: Force is a push or pull. Balanced forces (equal and opposite) result in no motion.
The Area Effect: Pressure = Force ÷ Area. Small area creates high pressure; large area creates low pressure.
Fluid Pressure: Liquids exert more pressure as depth increases. Atmospheric pressure decreases as altitude increases.
